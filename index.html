About

George is a native Californian, raised in the Salinas Valley. He later moved to Chico for school and has settled in the Central Valley. George has worked for the top retail and vacation destination companies in the county. George is now a third-generation insurance agent, following in the footsteps of his father, two uncles, and grandfather.

In 2015, he decided it was time to join the family ranks and leave the corporate world behind. Juggling a retail manager job and working on getting his company up and running, everything came to fruition in mid-2016 when George Beach Insurance Services LLC accepted its first client. Away from the office, George enjoys traveling, the occasional theme park visit, and exploring new wineries up and down California’s Central Coast.
